Battery Warranty Policy

Proration is calculated using total days between the original purchase date and replacement date divided by 30.

Tier determination is based on (Total Days ÷ 30).

  • 0–24 months (0–720 days): Free Replacement
  • 25–32 months (721–960 days): 25% of Battery Cost + Installation Labor
  • 33–50 months (961–1500 days): 50% of Battery Cost + Installation Labor
  • 51–84 months (1501–2520 days): 75% of Battery Cost + Installation Labor
